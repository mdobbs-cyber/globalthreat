## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, please do NOT open an issue.

Instead, please email the maintainers directly. We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours.

Please include:
*   A description of the vulnerability.
*   Steps to reproduce the issue.
*   Any relevant logs or screenshots.

We will work with you to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
